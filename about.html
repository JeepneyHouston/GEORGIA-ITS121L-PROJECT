About Us

Discover the passion behind Cheveron7 and the story of its creator.

Meet Liane

Hi, my name is Liane, and I aspire to have my own business. I am the creator of Cheveron7, a nail service business I started in May of last year. My passion for nail art goes beyond just creating beautiful designs, it’s about giving people a space to express themselves and feel confident.

I’ve always been drawn to having quality painted nails but due to the pricing of the service, I could not always afford to have them done. Seeing a client love a set I’ve created is what drives me. That’s why I’m working towards becoming a licensed nail technician and business owner, so I that can continue to grow and offer even more for my clients.

My goal is to make high-quality nail services that are also affordable, ensuring everyone can enjoy a little self-care without breaking the bank.

Mission:

To provide high-quality, affordable nail art services in a welcoming space where clients can express their individuality and experience the confidence that comes from dedicated self-care.

Vision:

To create a haven where professional nail art is an accessible and empowering form of self-expression for everyone, eliminating the choice between affordability and exceptional quality.
